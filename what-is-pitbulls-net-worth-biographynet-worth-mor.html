    Who is Pitbull?

    Pitbull, an American rapper, singer, songwriter, entrepreneur, and actor, was given the name Armando Christian Pérez in 1981. He started recording songs for several labels in the early 2000s, including reggaeton, Latin hip hop, and crunk.

    In 2004, TVT Records issued his debut album, M.I.A.M.I. El Mariel, The Boatlift, Rebelution, and Planet Pit are among the albums he has released since. “I Know You Want Me (Calle Ocho),” his first hit song, reached at number two on the US Billboard Hot 100 and number four on the UK Singles Chart.
