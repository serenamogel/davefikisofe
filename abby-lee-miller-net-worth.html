    Abby Lee Miller Wiki Biography

    Abby Lee Miller was born on the 21st September 1966, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania USA. She is a choreographer and dance instructor; Reign Dance Productions is owned and run by Miller. Abby Lee Miller attracted widespread attention through participation in the reality television show “Dance Moms” (2011–present) aired on Lifetime Network. In short, dancing is one of the main sources of Abby Lee Miller’s net worth and popularity.

    So just how rich is this choreographer? Under the latest estimations, it has been reported that the total size of Abby Lee Miller’s net worth is as much as $2 million. It should be noted that Miller was bankrupt with debts of $400,000 at the end of 2010.

    Abby Lee Miller was born to parents Maryen Lorrain Miller and George L Miller; her mother owned a self-named dance studio, so unsurprisingly Abby followed her mother’s footsteps and studied choreography and dance. While still studying at high school she played the clarinet, and attended ski and charm clubs, however, soon she realised that she was better at creating dances and coaching dancers than actually performing, and so founded her own self-named dance studio. What is most surprising is that at that time she was still only 14 years old. Later, Miller united with her mother’s dance studios, and they were re-named Reign Dance Productions (1995-present).

    However, the most significant source of Abby Lee Miller’s wealth has been the television show “Dance Moms” (2011–present). The show is focused on competitions of former and current student dancers guided by Abby Lee Miller. Although her teaching and motivating methods are sometimes criticized as being too aggressive, or even psychologically abusive, the show has high ratings. In fact, it has been nominated for a number of awards, winning three of them – BMI Cable Award, Dancers’ Choice Award and Kids’ Choice Award.

    Following the popularity of the show, three spin-offs Hve been released: “Abby’s Ultimate Dance Competition” (2012–2013), “Dance Moms: Miami” (2012) and “Dance Moms: Abby’s Studio Rescue” (2014). Afterwards, Dance Moms went to Los Angeles and continues their work there. These programs added considerably to Miller’s net worth.

    In addition to these projects, Abby Lee Miller appeared on the reality competition show “Dancing with the Stars” (2014) as a guest judge. Miller is also the author of the book “Everything I Learned about Life, I Learned in Dance Class” (2014). These also increased the net worth of Abby Lee Miller.

    In spite of fame and popularity, Miller experienced some legal problems because of her behaviour, with the family of one student, Paige Hyland, going to court alleging bullying and asking for $5 million as compensation; however, the charges against Abby Lee Miller were dropped.

    Concerning her private life, it was announced that Abby Lee Miller is engaged to Michael Padula. However, some sources claim that Michael is gay and that the engagement is only a hoax, whereas others say that the couple is already preparing for their wedding reception.
