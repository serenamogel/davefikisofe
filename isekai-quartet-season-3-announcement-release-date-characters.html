    Isekai Quartet is a Japanese anime series that is a chibi presentation style crossover between many light novel series like Overlord, Konosuba, Re: Zero, and The Saga of Tanya the Evil franchises. The anime series was directed and portrayed by Ashina Minoru. These series were are all distributed by Kadokawa Corporations while Studio Puyukai animated the series.

    It made its debut on 9 April 2019 comprising of 12 episodes followed by a second season on 14 January 2020 including enumerated guest characters from animes such as The Rising of the Shield Hero's Raphtalia and Naofumi, The Cautious Hero: The Hero is Overpowered but Overly Cautious' Seiya and Ristarte.

    With the completion of the serialization of Isekai Quartet Season 2 on 31 March 2020, the third season of the anime series is making its way soon.

    Announcement Of Isekai Quartet Season 3

    Isekai Quartet Season 3 has been announced to include new animes series such as BOFURI's Maple and Sally, Plunderer's Licht and Hina, and many more. The official Twitter of Isekai Quartet stated that the anime is currently under progress.

    (©: Twitter/Iseaki Quartet)

    Isekai Quartet revolves on the characters based on Overlord, Re: Zero, The Saga of Tanya the Evil, and Konosuba, where they are accidentally transported into a parallel world. The cause of their deporting into the parallel world is due to a magic button when it suddenly appears, and all the protagonists accidentally press the button, which shifts them towards a whole new world where another comedy, fun-based new Isekai story takes place featuring high school life.

    The red button touched by several characters of different anime led them in their journey to this parallel world. Now these characters who have been transported must be in the same class where fighting is strictly forbidden and getting to know each other adequately alongside listening to a senile teacher per the instructions upheld by the school rules.

    Isekai Quartet focuses solely on chibi anime characters based on hilarious comedy.

    Isekai Quartet Season 3 Release Date

    According to the producer Ashina Minoru, he had a hard time elaborating on the script and scenario of Isekai Quartet, which turned out to be a lot more complicated than he had artistically anticipated. In designing the series, he was supported by creators and producers of the original anime authors. Due to the struggles, hardships, and the love for his work, Minoru announced that he would like to issue and release the new sequel of the Isekai Quartet.

    The chibi anime received good support, and many fans excitedly gave great reactions from the first two seasons. With that consideration taken seriously, Minoru now focuses on creating an interesting Isekai Quartet Season 3 with many more new characters and storylines, which will make the viewers more interested in this chibi based parallel world lined with many animes together.

    The release date of the Isekai Quartet season 3 is not officially announced yet. However, some sources claim that the anime series will apparently make its return in January 2021.

    Isekai Quartet Anime And Characters Involved

    (©: Funimation)

    Isekai Quartet is the mix of several anime crossovers. These are the anime names and characters that involve in the anime Isekai Quartet.

    •Overlord - Ainz Ooal Gown is the supreme king of death and ruler of the great tomb of Nazarick. He is an undead who leads an army of many varieties of monsters. His main goal is to find his teammates while accidentally setting his aim for world domination. While accompanied by his floor guardians and maids, he works his way to become the true ruler of the world where he was transported. In Isekai Quartet, he is also accompanied by his floor guardians members such as Shalltear, Demiurge, Albedo, many more alongside with his trusty maids.

    •RE: Zero - Subaru Natsuki is the protagonist of the series Re: Zero Starting Another Life in Another World. He is a 17-year-old loner and maverick while being a gamer who abruptly finds himself deported to another world on his way back home from the appliance store. After venturing the town he was transported into, he meets a silver-haired half-elf girl named Emilia and happens to fall profoundly in love with her. Upon his entry in the Isekai world, Subaru procures an ability which he calls "Return by Death," that enables him to go back in the past when he dies, while still maintaining his memories of the previous timeline. In Isekai Quartet, Subaru, Emilia, Rem, Ram, and Beatrice are transported into a parallel world where they meet many characters whom they have never met.

    •Konosuba - Kazuma Satou is the main protagonist for this anime series. He is a 17-year-old shut-in who likes to coop up in his room to play games. While on his way home after purchasing a video game, he endeavors to save a classmate from a slow-moving tractor whom he mistakes as a fast-moving vehicle and dies due to an acute stress reaction. As an outcome, he is granted another life where he is sent into a parallel world where magic is available and becomes a role-playing character accompanied by the goddess Aqua whom he takes as a partner. He meets many allies throughout the series. In Isekai Quartet, Kazuma, alongside Aqua, Megumin and Darkness area transported into the parallel world.

    •Tanya The Evil - Tanya Von Degurechaff is the main protagonist for this series known as Saga of Tanya, the Evil. Tanya is a youthful girl with blonde hair, blue-eyes who gets reincarnated as a cold-hearted, materialistic, and civil Darwinistic- Japanese businessman. She acquires the skills of a mage with enchanting powers that enables her to fly and shoot bursting bullets from her guns. In Isekai Quartet, Tanya, alongside her battalion, joins the fray.

    •Rising of the Shield Hero - Naofumi Iwatani is the main protagonist of this anime series, while Raphtalia is the main heroine. He was transported into a world where he was shown as a chosen hero but afterward got betrayed by many people who turned him into a cruel, ruthless person. After meeting with Raphtalia, he slowly changes, but his hatred for the people who have shunned him will never disappear. Naofumi, alongside Raphtalia, joins the Isekai Quartet in Season 3. 

    •The Saga of Tanya the Evil - General Rudersdorf is the director of this parallel anime school alongside assisted by Zettour as his assistant.

    •Cautious Hero - Seiya, and Ristarte

    •Plunderer - Licht, and Hina
