    Kylie Jenner was born on August 10, 1997 who is very popular American socialite, media personality, and entrepreneur. She became famous by her appearances on the reality TV series i.e., Keeping Up with the Kardashians (2007-2021) later started to appear in The Kardashians which airs on Hulu from 2022. Kylie is the founder and owner of Kylie Cosmetics a successful cosmetics company with the fifth-largest following on Instagram.

    At the age of 14 years, Kylie collaborated with her sister Kendall to create a clothing line for PacSun called Kendall & Kylie.

    In 2015, she launched Kylie Lip Kits, later rebranded as Kylie Cosmetics in 2016.

    Kylie’s cultural influence began in the mid-2010s, with Time magazine recognizing her and Kendall as influential teens in 2014 and 2015.

    In 2017, she became the youngest person featured on Forbes Celebrity 100 list. She also starred in her own spin-off series i.e., Life of Kylie (2017).

    When Forbes estimated Kylie’s net worth at $1 billion in 2019, it made her the world’s youngest self-made billionaire at age 21.

    Personal Life

    Kylie Jenner has Dutch, English, Irish, Scottish, and Welsh ancestry. She has eight half-siblings on both sides of her family, including an older sister named Kendall.

    In 2015, her parents divorced. Kylie attended Sierra Canyon School and later finished high school at home, receiving her diploma from Laurel Springs School in Ojai, California, in July 2015.
