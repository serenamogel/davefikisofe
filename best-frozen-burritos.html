    4 Best Frozen Burritos, According to Taste Tests

    11 Jul 2024, 00:00

    The best frozen burritos have high highs and low lows. Some are so incredibly delicious and some are absolutely vile. We ate the entire spectrum of frozen burritos—from chicken, to bean, to beef, to breakfast—to find the best frozen burrito for you.

    It is with much heartburn that I confidently say this: After trying many, many, many frozen burritos brands, we have found the best frozen burritos. Whether you’re a culinarily-challenged bachelor looking for frozen breakfast burritos to start his morning or a hungry vegan looking for a quick and easy plant-based meal, we have something on this list for your tastes. I love every frozen burrito on this list and I know you will too!

    Best Breakfast Burrito

    Tina’s Breakfast Burrito, Handcrafted, Egg, Sausage & Cheddar CheeseInstacart

    This is like a cheesier version of the McDonald’s sausage breakfast burrito that you know and love. It was easily the best frozen breakfast burrito we’ve ever tried. The sausage is peppery and a little smoky. It’s super cheesy and soft. It’s also the perfect size: not too big and not too small. This is the best frozen burrito to enjoy in the morning without ruining the rest of your day.

    Best Chicken Burrito

    Trader Joe’s Chicken Chili Verde Burritos

    Trader Joe’s knows what they’re doing when it comes to frozen chicken burritos and this chili verde version is the best overall frozen chicken burrito we tried. It’s zesty, bright, salty, and meaty. The chunks of chicken are coated in a delicious salsa verde and wrapped in a warm flour tortilla. If you buy one thing from Trader Joe’s, it should be these. They are some of the best frozen burritos money can buy!

    Best Bean Burrito

    Ramona’s Bean & Cheese BurritoVons

    I don’t know Ramona, but I love her! This bean and cheese burrito is so gooey and cheesy and filling. It makes me smile just thinking about it. If you’re someone who loves beans, this is the best frozen burrito for you. It really highlights them! And this burrito has so much flavor. In fact, it has more flavor than most of the meaty frozen burrito brands we tried in our taste tests. I love this frozen bean burrito!!!

    Best Beef

    Alpha Plant-Based Mexicali BurritoAlpha

    Yes. A vegan ground beef burrito is the best frozen beef burrito we tasted in our beef burrito taste test. I don’t know what you want me to tell you! Most frozen beef burritos are filled with gray beef that feels like it might not even be beef…? That’s why we’re such fans of this meatless beef-style-crumble-filled burrito. The filling is very cheesy, a little spicy, and a bit tangy. This burrito also cooked up better in the microwave than any of the other frozen beef burritos we tried—the tortilla didn’t get all hard and dried out. If you’re willing to give fake beef a try (which you should be), give this burrito a shot! I promise it is actually one of the best frozen burritos you can find.
