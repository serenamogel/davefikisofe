    How do you make white concrete powder?

    To make white concrete powder, place 4 sand, 4 gravel and 1 white dye in the 3×3 crafting grid.

    What is concrete powder?

    Concrete Powder is a craftable block in Minecraft that turns into Concrete Blocks when it comes into contact with water. Concrete comes in 16 different colors depending on which dye a player uses when crafting Concrete Powder, and it is crafted with sand, gravel, and dyes.

    How do you make colored concrete powder in Minecraft?

    Concrete powder is crafted by placing 4 sand, 4 gravel, and 1 color dye into a crafting table.

    How do you make concrete not fall?

    Additional Information
  • Concrete needs to be mined with a pickaxe or else the block will be lost.
  • Similar to Sand and Gravel, Concrete Powder will obey gravity and fall if there is nothing below.
  • Water from rain, cauldrons, or water bottles will not harden Concrete.

    • How do you keep concrete powder from falling down?

  • In 1-deep water, place a block of concrete powder. Stack about 64 more concrete powder on top of it.
  • In survival mode, mine the bottom concrete block with a pickaxe, and keep using the pickaxe as new blocks of powder fall into its place.
