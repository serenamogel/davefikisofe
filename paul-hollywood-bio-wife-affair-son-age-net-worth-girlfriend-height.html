    Celebrity chefs bring a tray of candy with them everywhere they go. Not only are they constantly improving their skills, but they also present their delicacies to the delight of viewers around the world. Paul Hollywood is a British celebrity chef and television star who grew up in a succession of bakers. He is best known as a judge on The Great Brits Bake Off and also for his motorsport career.

    Paul Hollywood’s Bio and Age

    Paul Hollywood was born on the 1st of March 1966 in the town of Wallasey, situated in the Borough of Wirral, Cheshire, England. Born Paul John Hollywood, he is the son of bakery owner John F. Hollywood and Gillian M. Harman. He attended secondary school at Mosslands School. For college, he attended the Wallasey School of Art to study sculpture. However, he dropped out to work for his father instead.

    He started out at the headquarters of BreadWinner – his father’s bakery in York, before working in other bakeries around Merseyside. His baking skills helped him to work as head baker at a number of hotels in Britain including the Cliveden Hotel, the Dorchester and Chester Grosvenor and Spa. Paul Hollywood moved to Cyprus with his bakery career. There he worked at the Anassa Hotel and the Annabelle Hotel, both in Paphos.

    career

    Paul Hollywood is no longer a top baker’s prestige resorts and hotels turned media personality having a string of guest appearances on various television shows that have propelled the baker to widespread fame. He has appeared on BBC One’s The Heaven and Earth Show and ITV’s The Alan Titchmarsh Show . Paul Hollywood is one of the judges on The Great Brits Bake Off and has been a judge since the show’s inception in 2010. He has been praised for his straightforward way of judging, only judging the finished product and nothing else. Before she left the show in 2016, Mary Berry and Paul Hollywood were described as having one of the best ratings combos in television anywhere.

    His 2005 book 100 Great Loaves won the Gourmand World Cookbook Awards for TopBread and Pastry Book 2005. In 2008, Paul Hollywood created Britain’s Most Expensive Bread. Made from almonds and Roquefort, the baker calls his pastries the Rolls-Royce of breads. He has appeared as a guest on The Graham Norton Show and The One Show . He was also the host of his own programs; Paul Hollywood’s Bread on BBC Two, Paul Hollywood’s Pies and Puds on BBC One and Paul Hollywood’s Big Continental Road Trip , among others. Beginning in May 2013, he appeared on CBS ‘s The American Baking Competitionas a judge. He was also a judge on The Great American Bake Show , an American incarnation of The Great Britons Bake Off, from December 2012 .

    About Paul Hollywood’s wife, son and friend

    While he was the head baker at a hotel in Cyprus, Paul Hollywood met a diving instructor named Alexandra. They started dating in 1998 and tied the knot. In October 2001, the couple welcomed their son Josh. Things got tricky when rumors and reports circulated that Paul Hollywood was having an affair with The American Bake Competition co-host Marcela Valladolid. He would confirm the extramarital affair, and the couple separated in 2013. However, Paul Hollywood was able to end the affair and return with his wife Alexandra. Both Paul and his wife would confirm to the public that they are working things out.

    In 2017, pictures of Paul Hollywood and former British Bake Out winner Candice Brown made the rounds amid new rumors of infidelity. Paul and Candice both denied the allegations, but in the end Paul and his wife later announced they were getting a divorce. As of November 20th , 2017 , the divorce was finalized. Reports say that Paul’s growing fame continued to strain the marriage and the damage from his first fling was never really repaired.

    Since the divorce, there has been talk of PaulHollywood has a new girlfriend. Pictures online have shown Paul enjoying himself with Summer Moneys-Fullam, a 22-year-old barmaid at a local pub in Ickham, Kent, where Paul lives.

    Paul Hollywood Net Worth (Income and Earnings)

    Paul Hollywood has had a very successful career as a professional baker and television personality. His decades as a respected baker, as well as his companies – HJP Media LLP and Paul Hollywood Limited – earned him an estimated net worth of £10 million (US$12.7 million). His racing career and love of cars means he has some enjoyable drives. He owns an award-winning Aston Martin DB9 valued at £135,000 (approx. $172,000).

    Height and other facts

    Paul Hollywood stands at a height of 5 inches 10 feet. He is a passionate fan of the British football club Liverpool and attends the games at the home stadium Anfield. His former co-host Mary Berry is an Everton fan and the two have been known to trade banter across their clubs Bake Off .
