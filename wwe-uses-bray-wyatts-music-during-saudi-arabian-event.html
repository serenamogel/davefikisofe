    WWE Uses Bray Wyatts Music During Saudi Arabian Event

    Bray Wyatt hasnt been around since the road to WrestleMania, and his feud with Bobby Lashley was nixed suddenly. Now, we are months away from when we expected to see him again, and the Eater of Worlds is still not cleared for a return to the ring.

    06 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Bray Wyatt hasn’t been around since the road to WrestleMania, and his feud with Bobby Lashley was nixed suddenly. Now, we are months away from when we expected to see him again, and the Eater of Worlds is still not cleared for a return to the ring.

    BWE replied to a fan video of WWE playing Bray Wyatt’s Fiend music at the WWE Fan Fest in Saudi Arabia. The bona fide WWE insider said, “I wasnt there. But hearing it seems like the song is added audio to the original vid.”

    Another fan asked what WWE was doing by using Bray Wyatt’s music. WWE can use Bray Wyatt’s theme song however they want to, so it might have just been on the playlist.

    The fiend character is fully registered under WWE. They can use it whenever. So i have no doubts that Bray could use it

    This is interesting, because WWE is still keeping Bray Wyatt’s WWE music around. That being said, his music is also impossible to forget.

    Only time will tell how much longer it takes for Bray Wyatt to make a return, but the fact that his music playing at a Fan Fest in Saudi Arabia can cause this kind of fuss should tell you something about his influence.
