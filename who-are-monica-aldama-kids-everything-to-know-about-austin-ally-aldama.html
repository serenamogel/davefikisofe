    Who Are Monica Aldama Kids? She is a cheerleading coach and Tv personality and well known for the Netflix docuseries Cheer. 

    Monica Aldama is a T.V. personality famous for the Netflix docuseries Cheer, which chronicles the struggles and dynamics of the 2019 Navarro College coed cheerleading team. 

    Monica graduated from Corsicana High School, and after that, she joined the cheerleading squad at Tyler Junior College.

    Along with this, she obtained a B.B.A degree in Finance from the University of Texas at Austin. After that, she pursues her Master of Business Administration degree from the University of Texas at Tyler.

    As per the reports, she began coaching the cheer team of the Navarro College Bulldogs in the year 1955. Under guidance, the squads have won 14 National Championships in their division since 2000.

    Monica Aldama Kids – Meet Her Children Austin & Ally Aldama

    Monica Aldama and her husband Chris Aldama have two children together, son Austin and daughter Ally Aldama.

    Her son is a 25-year-old graduate of Texas Tech University, and he often accompanies his mother on the cheer team tours.

    Meanwhile, her daughter is 21 years old, and she is a student of Southern Methodist University in Dallas, Texas.

    Whereas she also followed in her mother’s footsteps and became a part of the cheerleading squad at her university.

    Monica Aldama Husband

    Monica Aldama was married to Chris Aldama for almost 30 years before becoming the head coach of the Navarro Bulldogs cheer team. Both met in middle school and after dating throughout their school days.

    After dating each other, both the partners tied the knot in January 1994. Her husband graduated from Southwest Texas State University with a Bachelor’s degree in Criminal Justice.

    He first worked as the Chief officer at the Ellis County Juvenile Services from 1994 to 2009.

    Currently, her spouse has been working at the Community Supervision and Corrections Department of Navarro County.

    In 2006, they hit the rough patch and even got divorced briefly. However, they reconciled their differences and remarried a year later.

    Meet Monica Aldama Instagram

    Monica Aldama is active on Instagram, and she often posts pictures and videos to update her fans. Her Instagram account is under her username @monicaaldama, and almost 632k people followed her.

    Frequently she shares sweet pictures of her family on social media and loves spending time outdoors with the children. Her fans hope that things will always remain this pleasant towards this tv personality.
