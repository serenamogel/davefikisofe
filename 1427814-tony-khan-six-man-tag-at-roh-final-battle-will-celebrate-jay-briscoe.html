    27 May 2024, 00:00

    Tony Khan comments on the star-studded Fight Without Honor at ROH Final Battle.

    In a tweet, Tony Khan confirmed that Bryan Danielson, Jon Moxley, and Claudio Castagnoli will face Mark Briscoe and FTR in a Fight Without Honor at ROH Final Battle. He stated that the match will celebrate the life of the late, great Jay Briscoe, who passed away in a car accident on January 17, 2023.

    Khan wrote that the match will also celebrate the anniversary of the Briscoes’ Dog Collar match against FTR. as well as the 20-year anniversary of Jay Briscoe’s match against Bryan Danielson at Final Battle 2003.

    ROH commentator Ian Riccaboni noted that this will be the first time Jon Moxley has a televised match in an ROH ring. He stated that, from 2007 to 209, Moxley wrestled three times for ROH, but they were never televised or shown on DVD.

    WrestleZone will have coverage of ROH Final Battle as it airs on December 15.
